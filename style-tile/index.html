A Heading

Kaiten-sushi is a sushi restaurant where the plates with the sushi are placed on a rotating conveyor belt that winds through the restaurant and moves past every table and counter seat. In South Korea, conveyor belt sushi has become popular and is known as “revolving sushi”. Also called sushi-go-round, mainly by foreigners living in Japan, is the popular English translation for Japanese fast-food sushi.
